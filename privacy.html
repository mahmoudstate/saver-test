Privacy Policy for Saver

Last Updated: May 26, 2026

At Saver, we believe that your financial information is yours alone. This Privacy Policy is designed to be transparent about our commitment to your privacy.

1. Data Collection & Storage

Saver is an offline-first application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All financial data—including your income, expenses, and savings goals—is stored exclusively on your device's local storage.

2. Data Sharing

Because we do not collect any data, we do not share, sell, or disclose your information to any third parties or external servers. Your data never leaves your device.

3. Permissions

The app may require standard access to your device's local storage to save your entries. It does not access your contacts, camera, photos, location, or any other sensitive personal data.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy practices, please contact us at: hello@savertrack.app
