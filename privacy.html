Privacy Policy

Saver One V1.0

At Saver, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how your data is handled with complete transparency.

◈

1. 100% Offline & Local Storage

Saver is designed to be a privacy-first app. All your financial records, accounts, transactions, and budgets are stored directly and exclusively on your local device. We do not use external cloud servers.

🔒

2. Zero Data Collection

We do not collect, log, or store any of your personal or financial information. Your data never leaves your device, and we have absolutely no access to it.

🚫

3. No Third-Party Tracking

Saver does not integrate with any third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking tools. Your financial habits remain 100% your own business.

⚙

4. Your Control, Your Data

Since all data lives on your device, you are in full control. You can backup your data via a JSON file or wipe it completely at any time through the application settings.

✉
